Converts a position from world space to screen pixel space.

>>> pos_world_to_screen((0, 0), (800, 800), (400, 400))
(200, 200)
>>> pos_world_to_screen((-400, -400), (800, 800), (400, 400))
(0, 400)
>>>

Converts sizes from BZFlag world space to screen space.
